Privacy Policy

Porta Angusta — The Narrow Gate · KJV Bible

Porta Angusta does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any remote server. No analytics, no telemetry, no advertising identifiers, no third-party SDKs. The app makes no network requests of any kind once installed.

What stays on your device

Anything you save inside the app — bookmarks, pinned searches, journal entries, your chosen text size, and your daily-verse preference — is stored only on your device, in iOS's local application storage. Uninstalling the app deletes that data.

Microphone & speech recognition

The microphone permission is requested only if you choose to use the Journal's optional voice-dictation feature. Audio is processed by Apple's on-device speech-recognition framework. Porta Angusta does not record audio to a file, does not transmit audio off the device, and does not retain any transcripts beyond what you choose to type into a journal entry.

The King James Version text

The complete text of the King James Version (Authorized Version) of the Bible ships inside the app as a CSV file. The KJV is in the public domain in the United States. In the United Kingdom it remains under perpetual Crown copyright administered by Cambridge University Press; non-commercial personal use is generally permitted.

Children

Porta Angusta is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content. It collects no information from any user, including children under 13, because it collects no information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will appear on this page with a new "Last updated" date below. Any change will preserve the app's no-data-collection posture; expansions of data handling would require a new app version submitted to App Review.

Contact

Questions about this policy or about the app's data handling can be sent to onetracksystem@outlook.com.
