Porta Angusta

The Narrow Gate · King James Version

"Enter ye in at the strait gate… because strait is the gate, and narrow is the way, which leadeth unto life, and few there be that find it." — Matthew 7:13–14

Porta Angusta is a King James Bible reader for people who want to read the Word carefully — and want their tools to keep up. The whole Authorized Version, 31,102 verses, every word, lives inside the app. No login, no network, no tracking.

What's inside

Read
Book and chapter navigation, adjustable text size, dark theme. Tap to highlight, double-tap to hear aloud. Read a whole chapter or just the verses you've selected.
Search
Type any word or reference. A live statistics panel breaks every search into Old Testament vs New Testament, then drills into per-book and per-chapter counts. Tap any of them to filter. Pin searches you return to often.
People & Places
Hundreds of named persons and places, automatically discovered from the KJV text. Each one has a summary card showing total mentions, distinct books, first and last reference, and the peak book — plus an arc diagram tracing the canonical journey. A study starting point, not a curated reference.
Journal
Voice or type your reflections. Attach one verse or many. Tap Reflect on any verse anywhere in the app to start or append a note. Search and re-read your own marginalia. Share entries to Messages, Mail, or anywhere the iOS share sheet reaches.

Privacy

There is no account. There is no telemetry. Your journal, bookmarks, and pinned searches live in this app only, on this device only. Read the full privacy policy →
